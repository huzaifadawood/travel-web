Beijing

Beijing, the capital of China, is a vibrant city known for its rich history and modern architecture. Home to the iconic Great Wall and the Forbidden City. The city is a hub for political, cultural, and educational activities in China. Its bustling markets, delicious cuisine, and historic sites make it a fascinating destination for travelers.

Shenzhen

Shenzhen has an extensive three-level public park system that was established in 2006, which categorizes parks as natural parks, urban parks, and community parks. By 2019, the city had 1,090 parks covering about 39,320 hectares, including 33 natural parks, 152 urban parks and 905 community parks.
